About Craibstone Golf

Marshall Leisure was formed in March 2011 by Charles R Marshall, a local businessman, to run and operate Craibstone Golf Course. Craibstone Golf Course clubhouse, AberdeenDespite its excellent location and design the course had never been given the chance to realise its full potential.

Naturally it is now the ambition of Marshall Leisure to remedy this and develop Craibstone into a top north-east golf course that its members can be proud of. The team behind Craibstone Golf Course consists of 11 motivated and experienced members of staff who are committed to fulfilling the ethos of the club; that golf should be a leisure pursuit.

Three members of staff form the highly qualified green-keeping team who constantly scrutinize the course to ensure it is in perfect condition throughout the season. The majority of the other staff form the catering team, which includes our own experienced chef supported by an attentive and enthusiastic serving team.
